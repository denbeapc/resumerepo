ABOUT ME

I have been programming ever since I was a freshman in high school. I had just beaten a video game I was playing, and the credits displayed the Java programming language logo. I was curious, and after some research, I ended the night writing my first "Hello World" program.

After spending many years exclusively coding in Java, I decided to make the switch to the C languages. I started off practicing C++ through making small ASCII video games. Soon after that, I found C# and have been coding in the .NET framework ever since.

I am an avid rock climber, and I love art. You can always find me near a mountain, on my computer, or at an art museum. I'm constantly trying to find ways to incorporate artistic inspiration into my projects.
