WHY I CHOSE BOOTCAMP

Due to my previous experience in programming and computer science, when I met my grandfather for the first time, he offered me a job. The only thing I had to do, was go through a bootcamp that would progress my skills in the areas that his work needed.

After weeks of research, and reading about numerous coding bootcamps, I had picked one. MAX Technical Training. They had the .NET program that I needed to be in, while also having a fantastic track record for preparing students for the outside world.
